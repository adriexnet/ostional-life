FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions from international buyers.

What is the price? What's included?
Lots start from $150,000. The purchase price includes water (ASADA Ostional) and electricity (Coopeguanacaste) service connections — no surprise costs on essential utilities. Contact us for exact pricing per lot.
How do I schedule a visit?
Message us on WhatsApp or call +506 8318-9195. We’ll confirm a time and meet you for a guided visit.
Is owner financing available?
Yes—owner financing may be available with reasonable terms. Contact us to discuss your timeline and options.
What utilities are available — and what's included in the price?
Water (ASADA Ostional) and electricity (Coopeguanacaste) connections are included in the purchase price. Mobile coverage via Kölbi and Claro. High-speed internet via Starlink.
Can foreigners buy property in Costa Rica?
Yes—foreigners can own property in Costa Rica with the same ownership rights as locals. We can share the recommended process and contacts.
What can I build on the lot?
The lots have mixed-use zoning and are ideal for a home with garden/orchard style living. We’ll share the practical guidelines and local considerations.
